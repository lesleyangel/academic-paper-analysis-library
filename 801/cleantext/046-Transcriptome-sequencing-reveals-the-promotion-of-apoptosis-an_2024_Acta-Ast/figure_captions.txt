Fig. 1. Schematic diagram of RPM system and cytological level analysis of BV2 cells in RPM. (A) Random positioning machine (RPM) system is an apparatus designed for simulating microgravity conditions on Earth. It achieves this through its dual-axis system, wherein each frame rotator is independently powered by its own electric motor. The RPM enables continuous and controlled rotation of biological samples along two orthogonal axes, effectively nullifying the net effect of gravitational forces by averaging out the gravity vector. For our experimental purposes, we utilized a specially designed cell culture flask measuring 30 mm in length, 20 mm in width, and 35 mm in height. This flask, which includes a silicone pad, a lid, and slots for slide insertion, has a total volume capacity of 15 mL, suitable for cell culture under simulated microgravity; (B) morphology and cell concentration of BV2 cells under a microscope at 20× and 40×; (C) viability of BV2 cells in RPM. Means ± SD are shown. Two-way ANOVA with unpaired Student’s t test was performed. *p < 0.05, **p < 0.01, ***p < 0.001 compared with control. N = 4 per each data point.

Fig. 2. Phenotypic change of BV2 cells in RPM. (A): BV2 cells were stained with CD86 and analyzed by flowcytometry; (B): BV2 cells were stained with CD80 and analyzed by flowcytometry.

Fig. 3. Apoptosis of BV2 cells induced in RPM. (A). BV2 cells were stained with FITC-conjugated Annexin V and PI, followed by the flow cytometric analysis; (B) the percentage of viable (Annexin V−/PI−), early apoptotic (Annexin V+/PI−), and late apoptotic/necrotic (Annexin V+/PI+) cells is summarized in the bar graph. All groups were compared with the 1 g control group (N = 3). Means ± SD are shown,*p < 0.05, **p < 0.01, ***p < 0.001.

Fig. 4. Differential expression of BV2 cells mRNA in RPM system. (A) The volcanic map obtained by visualizing the results of gene service under simulated microgravity in RPM system, which shows that the red color accords with p value < 0.01 and the log2foldchange >1, gray means neither of them fits, green and blue only fits one of them; (B) Differential genes under log2foldchange >1 and p value < 0.05 in heat map. The heat map of visual display of differential genes, red indicates increased expression of mRNA, blue indicates decreased expression of mRNA. Sequencing data from Illumina NovaSeq 6000, and the heat map is drawn by “pheatmap” package in R language. (For interpretation of the references to color in this figure legend, the reader is referred to the Web version of this article.)

Fig. 5. Enrichment and visualization of EDGs in RPM. (A) The GO enrichment pathways obtained by “enrichGO” package are displayed on the barplot for BP, CC and MF pathways respectively. The barplot is drawn by “ggplot2” package in R language; (B) the top six pathways of GO enrichment pathways are selected and displayed as chord graphs combined with corresponding genes. The chord graphs of the top six BP pathways represented by six colors, which drawn by “Gochord” package in R language; (C) bubble diagram shows the top 20 KEGG enrichment pathways. The change of bubble size corresponding to count and color shows the pvalue of enrichment pathway. Bubble diagram is drawn by “ggplot2” package in R language; (D) the histogram shows the top 10 results of KEGG enrichment pathway, in which the color change corresponds to the P. adjudt and the length shows the count. The barplot is drawn by the “clusterprofiler” package. (For interpretation of the references to color in this figure legend, the reader is referred to the Web version of this article.)

Fig. 6. The validation of 10 selected DEGs through qPCR. Relative mRNAs expression level was calculated by fold-change. Each group represented as the mean ± SD from three separate experiments (*p < 0.05).
